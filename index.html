Introduction

I'm Scott Kurz, a game designer with a passion for programming and game design. I love collaborating with others to find ways to create fun mechanics. Learning about the decisions that go into a game's mechanics is my favorite part of the game design process.

For more details, check out some of my previous work.

Untitled Dungeon Project

Untitled Dungeon Project is a short first-person puzzle game made in the Unreal Engine. Set in an underground prison, players are tasked with finding the treasure by navigating the environment and solving puzzles using a magical candle.

More About Me

I'm a graduating student majoring in Game Design and Development, and am currently based in Chicago. I graduated from Depaul University with a BS in Game Design. I have worked on a variety of game projects in a number of different game engines and development tools.

As a developer, I gain a lot of joy in the learning process of developing and testing mechanics and working with different teams. I love creating games which challenge players to think creatively. When I am not involved on a project, I am constantly learning more about the different parts of game development, teaching myself new techniques in areas like 3D modeling and animation, sound design, and programming. My passion is toying with new ideas and mechanics, and testing them to see how far they can go.

I'm always looking for new projects to get involved in. If you think my skills would be a good fit for you, contact me!
